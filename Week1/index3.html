This is a review of FrankenWeenie

"Frankenweenie" is a stop-motion animated film directed by Tim Burton. The story centers on young Victor Frankenstein, a boy who loves science and his pet dog, Sparky. When Sparky tragically passes away, Victor uses his scientific skills to bring him back to life in a Frankenstein-style experiment. However, his reanimated dog attracts the attention of neighbors and classmates, leading to both heartwarming and humorous moments as Victor tries to keep Sparky's resurrection a secret. The film combines Burton's signature dark and quirky style with a touching tale of the bond between a boy and his loyal companion, exploring themes of love, loss, and the consequences of playing with the forces of life and death.
